We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

This information may include:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number, address)

  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)
  • Project details and requirements

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior, including:

  • Device information (operating system, screen resolution)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our digital services
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, send updates, and provide customer support
  • Marketing: To send promotional materials, newsletters, and service announcements (with your consent)
  • Analytics: To understand how our website is used and optimize user experience
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activity

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating our business (e.g., hosting, payment processing, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee training on data protection

Important: While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to using reasonable measures to safeguard your data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information for certain purposes

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Ghana. We ensure that adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Manifest Digital uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and provide a better user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Authentication and security
  • Session management
  • Load balancing

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Page visit analytics
  • User behavior tracking
  • Error reporting

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality:

  • Language preferences
  • Remember your choices
  • Personalized content

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your online activity to deliver relevant advertising:

  • Ad targeting and retargeting
  • Campaign performance measurement
  • Social media integration

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use services from trusted third parties that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and insights
  • Facebook Pixel: For advertising and remarketing
  • LinkedIn Insights: For B2B marketing analytics
  • Hotjar: For user behavior analysis

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Clear cookies when closing the browser

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of specific tracking:

  • Google Analytics Opt-out: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Network Advertising Initiative: optout.networkadvertising.org

Note: Blocking or deleting cookies may affect your experience on our website and limit certain features.

5. Cookie Duration

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or regulations. Check this page periodically for updates.

Refund Policy

Last Updated: October 6, 2025

At Manifest Digital, we are committed to delivering high-quality digital solutions. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds and cancellations.

1. General Policy

Due to the custom nature of our digital services and the time invested in each project, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We strive for complete client satisfaction and will work with you to resolve any issues.

2. Consultation and Discovery Calls

  • Free Consultations: Our initial discovery calls and consultations are provided free of charge with no commitment required
  • Paid Consultations: If applicable, paid consultations are non-refundable once completed

3. Project-Based Services

Deposit Payments

  • Initial deposits are typically non-refundable as work begins immediately upon project initiation
  • Deposits cover initial planning, research, and resource allocation

Milestone Payments

  • Payments for completed milestones are non-refundable
  • If you're unsatisfied with a milestone, we'll work to revise it according to the agreed scope
  • Additional revisions beyond the agreed scope may incur extra charges

Project Cancellation

If you need to cancel a project:

  • Before Work Begins: Full refund minus any consultation fees and administrative costs (typically 10%)
  • After Work Begins: You'll be charged for work completed plus a 25% cancellation fee on remaining contract value
  • After 50% Completion: No refunds; payment for remaining milestones is due

4. Subscription Services

For ongoing subscription-based services:

  • Monthly Plans: Cancel anytime; no refund for current month but service continues until period end
  • Annual Plans: Cancel with 30 days notice; refund prorated for unused months minus 15% processing fee
  • Setup Fees: Non-refundable once service is configured

5. Training and Workshops

  • 7+ Days Before: Full refund available
  • 3-7 Days Before: 50% refund
  • Less Than 3 Days: No refund, but can transfer to another session
  • No-Show: No refund

6. Digital Products and Licenses

  • Software licenses and digital products are non-refundable once delivered
  • If a product is defective, we'll provide a replacement or fix at no charge
  • 30-day money-back guarantee applies to specific products (clearly marked)

7. Conditions for Refund Eligibility

Refund requests must:

  • Be submitted in writing to billing@manifestghana.com
  • Include detailed reasons for the refund request
  • Be made within 30 days of the disputed charge
  • Provide supporting documentation if applicable

Satisfaction Guarantee: While refunds are limited, we're committed to your satisfaction. If you're unhappy with our work, we'll revise it according to the original scope at no additional charge.

8. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds are processed within 10-15 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Bank processing times may vary (3-7 additional business days)
  • Transaction fees are non-refundable

9. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Change of mind after project completion
  • Failure to use delivered services or products
  • Dissatisfaction with results that meet the agreed specifications
  • Issues caused by third-party services or client-provided materials
  • Delays caused by client's failure to provide required information or approvals

10. Dispute Resolution

If you have a billing dispute:
