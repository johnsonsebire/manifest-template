Frequently Asked Questions

What should I prepare for the call?

Come with a brief description of your project goals, target audience, and any specific features you have in mind. If you have reference websites or designs, that's helpful too. Don't worry if you're not fully prepared—we'll guide you through the discovery process.

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, absolutely! All consultation calls are 100% free with no obligations. We believe in providing value upfront and building relationships based on trust. You'll receive honest advice and recommendations whether or not you decide to work with us.

What happens after the call?

After our call, you'll receive a detailed summary via email, including key discussion points, recommendations, and a custom proposal with transparent pricing. There's no pressure to proceed—take your time to review everything and reach out when you're ready.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Of course! Life happens. You can reschedule or cancel your appointment through the confirmation email you'll receive. We just ask for at least 24 hours notice so we can offer that time slot to someone else.

Which meeting type should I choose?

If you're just starting and exploring options, choose the Discovery Call. For projects with defined technical requirements, opt for Technical Consultation. If you're ready to move forward and need detailed planning, book a Project Discussion. Not sure? Start with Discovery—we can always schedule additional sessions.

What time zones do you support?

We work with clients globally and accommodate GMT, EST, and other major time zones. Our calendar displays available times in your local timezone automatically. We're flexible and can arrange special time slots for international clients when needed.
