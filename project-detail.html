KokoPlus: Transforming Coconut Commerce

A modern e-commerce platform that increased online sales by 250% and revolutionized how Ghanaians buy premium coconut products.

Client
KokoPlus Ghana
Industry
E-commerce / Retail
Timeline
12 Weeks
Team Size

Project Overview

KokoPlus, Ghana's leading coconut products retailer, approached us with a challenge: their existing website wasn't converting visitors into customers. With growing demand for their premium coconut oil, water, and other products, they needed a modern e-commerce platform that could scale with their ambitions.

We partnered with KokoPlus to design and develop a comprehensive e-commerce solution that not only looked beautiful but also delivered exceptional user experience and drove sales. The platform needed to handle high traffic volumes, integrate with local payment gateways, and provide seamless mobile shopping experience.

The result exceeded all expectations: a 250% increase in online sales within the first 6 months, 180% growth in mobile conversions, and a significant improvement in customer satisfaction scores.

The Challenge

Key obstacles we needed to overcome for KokoPlus

Low Conversion Rate

The existing website had a conversion rate of only 0.8%, significantly below industry standards. Visitors were leaving without making purchases.

Poor Mobile Experience

Over 75% of traffic came from mobile devices, but the site wasn't optimized for mobile shopping, leading to cart abandonment.

Payment Integration

Limited payment options and lack of integration with popular Ghanaian mobile money platforms were major barriers to purchase.

Logistics Complexity

No system for managing delivery zones, calculating shipping costs, or tracking orders in real-time across Greater Accra.

Slow Performance

Page load times exceeded 8 seconds, causing high bounce rates and negatively impacting SEO rankings.

Security Concerns

Lack of SSL encryption and outdated security measures made customers hesitant to enter payment information.

Our Solution

We developed a comprehensive e-commerce platform built on modern technology with a focus on user experience, performance, and conversion optimization.

Modern UI/UX Design

Intuitive navigation, clear product displays, and streamlined checkout process designed to maximize conversions.

Mobile-First Approach

Fully responsive design optimized for mobile shopping with touch-friendly interfaces and fast load times.

Multi-Payment Integration

Support for Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone), cards, and cash on delivery with secure payment processing.

Smart Shopping Cart

Persistent cart across devices, saved items, quick reorder, and intelligent product recommendations.

Advanced Search & Filter

Powerful search with autocomplete, category filters, and personalized product suggestions.

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time insights into sales, customer behavior, inventory levels, and business performance.

Delivery Management

Automated shipping calculations, delivery zone mapping, and real-time order tracking.

Notification System

Email and SMS notifications for order confirmations, shipping updates, and promotional campaigns.
